Who Are We?

We're a team of people who love to learn and discover new things.

Most of us have overloaded our eReaders and shelves with books. Some of us have probably collected more advanced degrees than necessary. A few of us have launched a couple of startups and learned a lot of things the hard way.

All of us believe that technology should make peoples’ lives better. Siminars is our humble attempt to do that by helping people share ideas and knowledge.
